Ribs

Description

This easy to follow recipe will have you mastering around the block famous fall off the bone ribs before you know it

Ingredients

  • Pork side, or back ribs
  • Favourite rub or spice blend (can't ever go wrong with SPG)
  • Spritz bottle with apple juice or ACV
  • Yellow mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat smoker to 225F
  2. Remove the silverskin off the underside of the ribs with a dull ended butter knife, this takes off that awful weird underskin that gets weird when cooking. You'll thank me later
  3. Spread mustard over both sides of the ribs, this will act as a binding agent for the rub to stick on to and create that to kill for bark
  4. Cover the ribs on both sides with a generous amount of rub
  5. Place ribs directly on the grill grates for 3 hours
  6. Spritz with the apple juice/ACV mixture every hour
  7. Wrap ribs in aluminum foil, be sure so pour a little of the spritz mix into each foil pack of ribs and cook for another two hours
  8. Remove ribs from foil and place on direct heat and cover in your favourite sauce and allow to set for 20-30 minutes
  9. Pull ribs from smoker and enjoy that fall off the bone goodness
