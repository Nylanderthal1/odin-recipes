Brisket

Description

The king of the bbq, its brisket, baby! Theres nothing quite like it, and when done properly is easily the one of most delicious and impressive dishes an aspiring pit master can well, master. This recipe is going to have you enjoying mouth watering brisket in no time!

Ingredients

  • 14-18LB whole packer brisket
  • Rub of your choice, (salt, pepper and garlic is always popular)
  • BBQ grade aluminum foil
  • Ice cold beer

Steps

  1. Preheat your smoker at 225F
  2. Trim excess fat off of your brisket, be sure to remove and hard fat that will not render down in the cooking process. If you want you can save the fat and trimmings and make some tallow.
  3. Rub a generous amount of your rub on both sides of the brisket, be sure to cover as much of the meat as you can as this will help create that delicious bark everyones is woofing about.
  4. Once smoker is at set tempurature place brisket directly on the grill grates FAT SIDE UP
  5. Crack open one of those ice cold beers and get settle in with your favourite Netflix series while the meat does its thing
  6. Once brisket reaches an IT of 160F, remove from smoker and place onto an aluminum foil boat allowing the meat to rest completely within the boat, but having the top exposed
  7. Crack another ice cold beer and settle back into that show you were watching
  8. When brisket reaches an IT of 205 remove from smoker, cover completely and allow to rest in a cooler packed with towels, or in an oven set to 150F for 1 to 2 hours
  9. Remove from towel wrap, slice and enjoy!
